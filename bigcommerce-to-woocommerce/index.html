Why Move from BigCommerce to WooCommerce?

BigCommerce works — but monthly plan costs keep rising, transaction fees eat your margin, and you're locked into Bigcommerce's hosting forever. WooCommerce gives you complete ownership, zero platform fees, and more flexibility.

  • 💰

    Dramatically Lower Total Cost

    BigCommerce Pro costs $299–$400+/month and increases as your revenue grows. WooCommerce is open-source. Most stores cut their platform costs by 70–85% after migration.

  • 🔧

    Simpler for Your Team to Manage

    WooCommerce runs on WordPress — the world's most popular CMS. Your marketing team can update products, content, and promotions without a developer.

  • 🚀

    Faster Performance Out of the Box

    WooCommerce on modern managed hosting (WP Engine, Kinsta, Cloudways) consistently matches BigCommerce performance at a fraction of the infrastructure cost.

  • 🔌

    Massive Plugin Ecosystem

    60,000+ WordPress plugins including Klaviyo, ReCharge, Yotpo, and every payment gateway you need — most available free or at low monthly cost.

Our Process

How We Handle Your BigCommerce Migration

A proven 5-step migration process that moves your BigCommerce store to WooCommerce — without losing a product, an order, or a Google ranking.

01

Free Store Audit

We audit your BigCommerce store — product count, custom apps, integrations, data complexity — and deliver a detailed migration scope and fixed-price quote within 24 hours.

02

WooCommerce Setup

We configure your new WooCommerce environment on a staging server — theme, payment gateways, shipping zones, and tax settings — before a single byte of data moves.

03

Data Migration

All BigCommerce products, customers, orders, content, and media are migrated using our proven toolkit. SEO redirects are configured in parallel to protect your Google rankings throughout.

04

Full store walkthrough on staging — checkout flow, payment processing, email notifications, inventory accuracy, and cross-device testing — before your BigCommerce store is touched.

05

Go-Live & Monitor

DNS cutover, final SEO verification, and 30 days of post-launch monitoring. Your BigCommerce store stays live until you're completely satisfied with the new WooCommerce store.
